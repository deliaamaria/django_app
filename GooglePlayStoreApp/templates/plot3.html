Rating depending on the year of release

This chart represents a time-series analysis of user reviews for applications, with data spanning from 2010 to 2021. Overall, the trend is upward, indicating a positive evolution of user ratings and the increasing quality and popularity of applications.

New and exciting technologies emerge, bringing forth useful and highly popular applications, along with a growing number of entertainment and social media apps with immense success. There is a notable dip in app ratings from 2018 to 2019, but the upward trend quickly resumes, especially due to the necessity of technology during the 2020-2021 period, when the pandemic emerged. During that time, the population needed various applications to maintain their daily activities at a level resembling what was before. As a result, applications related to education, sports, online conferencing, banking, and more experienced significant growth.
